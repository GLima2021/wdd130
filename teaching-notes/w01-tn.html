Week 01

Before the Lesson Begins

  • Students will have a preparation quiz in the Intro Week. Usually this would be done before the first day of class, but this week they will complete it before the second day of class.
  • Preparation Quiz results.  The students must score 100% on the Preparation quiz to move on. This is to encourage them to spend the time needed to really learn those terms. They can take it as many times as they need.  This will be the same next week with the preparation quiz.
  • Canvas might be a new experience for many students. You may want to lead them through a quick tour.
  • Remind students to plan their time so that they can work consistently on the course throughout the week.
  • Many students are fresh out of high school; make the expectations and standards clear to the students.

During the Lesson

  • Install Visual Studio Code and some extensions in class with the students. Here are some videos to guide you through the process.

  • Make sure students actually set up the aboutme folder with an index.html started, an images folder, a styles folder, and a main.css inside of the styles folder. This is demonstrated on the video.

  • Take some time during class to build a simple page together so they feel comfortable with the 'Getting to Know You' assignment. 

Assessments

  • Students will create their first html page all about them and place it on a discussion board this week for you and the other students to see.
