Week 09 - Complete the Whitewater Site

Before the Lesson Begins

  • This is a big week with two large deadlines. The students should complete their Rafting site and their site plan for their personal site. 

During the Lesson

  • There is an ICE Challenge this week to keep them fresh on grid. ICE example
  • Most of your time this week should be spent responding to questions and helping students solve HTML/CSS challenges with their child pages.
  • As students begin thinking about how to present their content and creating wireframes, I would introduce them to the 'How To' section at https://www.w3schools.com/howto/. They will have an opportunity to build a component like this in Week 12. 
  • Make sure you are available to students through office hours and make responses within 24 hours.
  • Check the Course Questions and Conversations Discussion Board.

Assessments

  • Review assignments and give feedback as needed.
