Week 05 - More CSS

Before the Lesson Begins

  • This week, students will continue learning HTML and CSS by looking at font properties and spacing. 
  •  They should have completed the preparation material and preparation quiz before any other assignment.

During the Lesson

  • The students should refer back to their site plans to remember which fonts they had chosen. The activity will refer back to fonts and other font properties introduced in week 2.
  • This week the students will also be asked to begin planning for their personal site. They should have chosen a topic and this week will prepare an elevator statement 'selling' their idea.
  • Students will cover concepts like fonts, box-model (padding, border, and margin). Students should feel comfortable using the browser developer tools during this coding. You may want to meet with them to practice with developer tools.

    W05 Quiz: Preparation

  • Students should finished the preparation videos and/or reading assignment and quiz at the first of the week. This ensures they are ready to jump into the hands-on exercises.

    W05 Box Model Practice

  • Students can practice with the box model by adding to the web page they have practiced with in previous weeks by following this Box Model Practice Example

    W05 Assignment: Web Challenge

  • Students will complete this week's Web challenge. Here is the actual HTML file for your reference. They should try and complete it by just recreating the page from the image. 

    W05 Assignment: Elevator Pitch

  • Student will settle on an idea for the personal website. This week they will communicate this idea through an elevator pitch. They will present their idea (give their elevator pitch) to others and get feedback from them about their site idea.

    W05 Assignment: CSS - Fonts and Spacing

  • Students will continue to work with their rafting home page and add different fonts and spacing to the page.
  • Again, remind them to read carefully and realize not all the exact code syntax is given to them. They need to read carefully and make sure they have the right code.

    W05 Student Feeback to Instructor

  • Students will complete a survey to give feedback to their instructor this week.
  • Make sure you are available to students through office hours and make responses within 24 hours.
  • Check the Course Questions and Conversations Discussion Board.
