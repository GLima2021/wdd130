Week 11 - Layout revisited

Before the Lesson Begins

  • The model for the rest of the semester will be the same. A short activity to teach some additional knowledge about HTML/CSS, and working on the personal sites.

During the Lesson

    W11 Assignment: Layout Revisited

  • We come back and spend more time on layout this week. The focus is to get the students to understand layout, both with Grid and without. 
  • They will complete all 28 levels at cssgridgarden.com and submit a screen shot to show their completion.
  • The second part of this assignment demonstrates laying out a page without grid. It shows that grid is not always needed for all layout. It also gives them hands-on practice with the nth-of-type pseudo-selector to demonstrate that classes and id's don't always have to be used for multiple elements.
  • This has been a confusing assignment for students so there is a video at the bottom of step 2 showing a very similar process.
  • Student will submit a screen shot of the poem layout for the second screen shot to submit.

    W11 Assignment: Personal Site Home Page

  • This week the students are asked to write the CSS for their home page. This will most likely generate a lot more questions than last week.
