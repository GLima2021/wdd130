Sugar, whem is too much? How we can help our kids from it?

Certainly, here's a shorter version without the stars: Sugar, When Is It Too Much and How to Help Kids: - Limit Sugar: Children should consume less than 25 grams (6 teaspoons) of added sugars per day. - Health Risks: Excessive sugar can lead to obesity, diabetes, dental issues, and heart disease. - Tips to Reduce Sugar: - Check food labels for added sugars. - Avoid sugary drinks; opt for water, milk, or unsweetened beverages. - Offer healthy snacks like fruits, veggies, and nuts. - Cook at home to control sugar in meals. - Teach moderation and occasional treats. - Be a healthy eating role model for your kids.
