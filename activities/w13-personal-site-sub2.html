Personal Site Subpage 2

Last week you should have gotten the styling for your first subpage done. This week we will style the second Child page.

Activity Directions

  1. Open the file

    Open the file for your subpage.

  2. Child Page Grid

    Open the wireframe for your the child page. We need to build the Grid for your layout. From the wireframe, determine how many columns and rows you will need. Then create those with CSS. Then start placing your elements into the grid cells.

  3. Spacing and Alignment

    Add padding and margins where appropriate to give your layout the right amount of white-space.

    Adjust the alignment of elements as well at this time as needed.

  4. Link it up

    Make sure to update the navigation in your Home page, your subpage 1, and this page so that if you click on a link on any page, it will take you to the other.

    Note that this week you are finishing up the second subpage. You have one more week to make any adjustments needed for your entire site. Check past feedback for adjustments you may need to make. Next week is when the entire site and a report for the site will need to be complete.

  5. Publish

    Validate your HTML and CSS, fix any errors, then submit the URL to your site in ILearn. Make sure the URL takes the instructor to your second child page that is completed.
